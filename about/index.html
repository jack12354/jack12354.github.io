About Me

I'm Jack Baker. I live just outside of Boston, MA with my plants and too many ideas. My first language was trying to reverse engineer the BASIC programs that made up my math homework back in 7th grade, which I promptly forgot about until four years later, when I learned Java and enough TI-BASIC to not have to memorize my calculus theorems. I then moved on to learn C# and C++ and found my happy place (It's C# 6.0 for those counting at home.) I dabbled in a few other languages, but kept finding my way back to C# and C++.
